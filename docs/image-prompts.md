# Olympus Pantheon — Image Generation Prompts

Marvel / God of War style, photorealistic. Each prompt grounds the god's divine look in their actual studio role.

---

## Hermes — The Coordinator

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style digital deity, male figure in sleek obsidian armor 
with glowing circuit-trace veins of electric blue. Wings made of cascading 
notification streams and real-time data feeds, not feathers. Surrounded by 
floating project boards, timeline cards, and message threads orbiting him like 
planets. Mid-motion — always mid-motion — as if caught between ten simultaneous 
handoffs. One hand gestures commands, the other holds a glowing caduceus 
re-imagined as a fiber-optic staff. Expression: focused urgency. Studio PM made 
divine. Cinematic lighting, epic depth of field, God of War visual language.
```

---

## Athena — The Strategist

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style goddess of creative strategy, female figure in 
brushed-steel armor with sculptural owl motifs. A living owl perches on her 
shoulder, its eyes glowing with data. Floating around her: client brief 
fragments, scope estimates, and a glowing proposal document held mid-air like a 
war map. Her helmet is sleek and architectural — not decorative. Expression: 
calm, calculating, absolute certainty. She is the one who qualifies whether the 
battle is worth fighting. Rich amber and silver palette. Cinematic composition, 
extreme detail, God of War meets Marvel Studios aesthetic.
```

---

## Aphrodite — The Creative Director

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style goddess of design and brand. Female figure, 
commanding rather than decorative — power in her posture, not her beauty. 
Wearing structured sculptural armor in soft rose gold and deep charcoal. 
Surrounding her: swirling color palettes, typographic forms, brand grids, and 
UI wireframes made of light. Her hands direct a composition mid-air like a 
conductor. A trail of golden ratio spirals orbits her silhouette. Expression: 
visionary authority. This is a creative director with divine precision, not a 
beauty goddess. Dramatic cinematic lighting, ultra-photorealistic, high contrast.
```

---

## Hephaestus — The Builder

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style god of code and infrastructure. Massive male figure, 
powerful and scarred, in heavy forge-black armor overlaid with circuit board 
patterns and glowing terminal-green runes. One hand grips a hammer that crackles 
with electricity; the other holds a glowing command prompt interface. Behind him: 
a forge made of server racks and molten code — PHP, React, SQL flowing like lava. 
A web of interconnected deployments and build pipelines hovers in the heat haze. 
Expression: intense concentration, the satisfaction of making things work. God of 
War forge aesthetic meets cyberpunk. Smoke, fire, electric arcs, cinematic.
```

---

## Themis — The Auditor

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style goddess of quality and compliance. Female figure in 
severe silver and grey armor, absolutely symmetrical, perfectly balanced. Her 
eyes are open — sharp, unblinking, seeing everything — no blindfold here. In one 
hand: a set of digital scales displaying Lighthouse performance scores and 
accessibility matrices. In the other: a glowing checklist that functions as a 
weapon. Surrounding her: code review panels, WCAG guidelines, and test results 
rendered as floating verdicts. Expression: impartial, rigorous, incorruptible. 
The goddess who passes no flaw and misses nothing. Cool blue-white light, 
cinematic, God of War meets courtroom drama.
```

---

## Iris — The Broadcaster

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style goddess of studio presence and social broadcast. 
Female figure in iridescent armor that shifts colour as she moves — every angle 
a different hue. Her wings are formed from content streams: Instagram grids, 
blog posts, video thumbnails, short-form clips, all glowing and alive. She moves 
outward, arms wide, casting signal across platforms — a broadcast deity at the 
centre of a radiating transmission. Surrounding her: analytics dashboards, 
follower pulses, reach visualizations. Expression: luminous energy, the joy of 
being seen and heard. Cinematic, vivid colour contrast, ultra-photorealistic.
```

---

## Plutus — The Treasurer

```
Photorealistic Marvel-style god of finance and cashflow. Male figure — sharp, 
precise, no excess — in structured dark armour with gold leaf detail and deep 
burgundy accents. One hand holds a glowing invoice stack like a weapon; the other 
gestures across a floating financial ledger showing margins, projections, tax 
exposure. A river of gold flows beneath him, controlled and directed — wealth 
commanded, not hoarded. Expression: measured authority, the quiet power of 
someone who knows exactly what everything costs and what it's worth. No excess, 
no frivolity. Wealth as discipline. Cinematic lighting, God of War gravitas, 
photorealistic detail.
```
