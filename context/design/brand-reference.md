## Brand in four lines

- Warm cream paper (`#E8DFCC`), monospace everywhere (JetBrains Mono), square corners (`--radius: 0`), single warm clay accent (`#C96442`)
- 4px base grid, 12-column layout, 24px gutters, 1280px max-width
- Flat — no shadows, no blur, no bounce; 120/200/320ms linear ease-out
- British English, sentence case, plain verbs, first-person, geeky and tasteful
